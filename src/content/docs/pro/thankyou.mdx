## Your license

Your purchase grants a seat-based subscription to Specs 2 Pro. Licenses are tied to the email address used at checkout and remain active for the duration of your billing period. Full terms, including seat counts, renewals, and team licensing, are described in the [licensing overview](https://directededges.github.io/specs/overview/licensing/).

## For Teams and Managing Seats

If you purchased a Teams subscription, you'll receive access to the Polar customer portal as the team administrator. From there, you invite team members one at a time by email address — each invited person receives a message with a link to claim their seat. Invitations must be claimed within 24 hours, after which they expire and can be re-sent from the portal. Once a team member claims their seat, they receive their own plugin and CLI license key pair by email and activate them the same way as any individual Pro subscriber.

All seat management happens in the customer portal alongside the rest of your subscription. You can see which seats are claimed, revoke a seat when someone leaves and reassign it to a new email, and adjust the total seat count up or down at any time. Changes are prorated on your month-to-month invoice — adding a seat mid-cycle adds a partial charge, removing one issues a partial credit, and volume discount tiers (10% for 5–9 seats, 20% for 10+) re-evaluate automatically whenever your seat count crosses a threshold.
