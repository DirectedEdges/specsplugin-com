## Your license

Your purchase grants a seat-based subscription to Specs 2 Pro. Licenses are tied to the email address used at checkout and remain active for the duration of your billing period. Full terms, including seat counts, renewals, and team licensing, are described in the [licensing overview](https://directededges.github.io/specs/overview/licensing/).

## Manage your subscription

Use the Polar customer portal to view receipts, update your payment method, change your billing email, or cancel your subscription. The portal is the same place you'll go to download invoices for expensing.
